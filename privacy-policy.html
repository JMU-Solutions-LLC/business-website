Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Immediatelyy
Last Updated: August 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how JMU Solutions LLC ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with our Discord bot(s), services, and website. This Policy applies to all bots and services developed and operated by JMU Solutions LLC ("Studio Bots") and also clarifies our role when building bots for clients ("Client Bots").

1. Scope of Application

• For Studio Bots, JMU Solutions LLC acts as both developer and operator. We collect and control data necessary to deliver services.
• For Client Bots, JMU Solutions LLC acts as the developer. Upon delivery, ownership and data control transfer to the client. If the client opts for hosting through JMU Solutions LLC, we act solely as a data processor under the client's instructions, and the client remains the data controller responsible for compliance with applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following types of data:
• Discord user ID, username, and channel/server IDs
• Invoice-related information (e.g., email address, order metadata)
• Support ticket activity or messages voluntarily submitted
• IP address (solely for tax calculation when paying invoices via Stripe)

3. Use of Information

We use your information to:
• Deliver requested services and maintain bot functionality
• Issue invoices and process payments through Stripe
• Determine applicable tax rates based on your location (IP address collected only for this purpose)
• Communicate with you regarding orders or support
• Ensure system security and integrity

4. Data Storage

Data is stored securely on servers maintained by JMU Solutions LLC. We also utilize trusted third-party providers such as Stripe (for billing/tax) and Cloudflare (for routing/protection). We do not store personal data on our public-facing website.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill orders, comply with legal obligations (including tax regulations), or resolve disputes.

5a. Hosting & Data Processing for Client Bots

If a client chooses to host their bot with JMU Solutions LLC, we process and store data on behalf of the client solely to provide hosting services. In these cases:
• The client remains the data controller and is responsible for establishing lawful bases for processing.
• JMU Solutions LLC acts as a data processor and processes data only under documented instructions from the client.
• We do not use or disclose client-hosted data for any purpose other than hosting, maintenance, and security.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting hello@jmusolutionsllc.com. We respond to valid requests within seven (7) days.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, though no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:
• Contractual Necessity: To provide and deliver services you request.
• Legal Obligation: To comply with tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements.
• Legitimate Interests: To maintain security, improve services, and prevent fraud.
• Consent: When you explicitly consent to optional data uses.

9. GDPR Rights (EEA Users)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the right to:
• Access your personal data.
• Request correction or deletion of your data.
• Restrict or object to our processing.
• Request data portability.
• Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@jmusolutionsllc.com.

10. CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
• Know what personal data we collect and how we use it.
• Request deletion of your personal data.
• Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal data (we do not sell data).
• Exercise these rights without discrimination.

To make a request under CCPA, contact us at hello@jmusolutionsllc.com.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers and third-party providers (e.g., Stripe, Cloudflare) are located. We implement safeguards consistent with GDPR, including standard contractual clauses where applicable.

12. Do Not Track

We do not track users across third-party websites for targeted advertising and do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals.
